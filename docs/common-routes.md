# Common Routes

## SLC to JFK

- Route: Salt Lake City to New York JFK
- Approximate great-circle distance: 1,990 miles
- Approximate distance in kilometers: 3,200 km
- Typical block time: around 4.5 to 5.5 hours depending on winds and traffic

## LAX to JFK

- Route: Los Angeles to New York JFK
- Approximate great-circle distance: 2,475 miles
- Approximate distance in kilometers: 3,983 km
- Typical block time: around 5.5 to 6.5 hours

## ATL to ORD

- Route: Atlanta to Chicago O'Hare
- Approximate great-circle distance: 606 miles
- Approximate distance in kilometers: 975 km
- Typical block time: around 2 hours

## DFW to DEN

- Route: Dallas Fort Worth to Denver
- Approximate great-circle distance: 641 miles
- Approximate distance in kilometers: 1,032 km
- Typical block time: around 2 hours

## KSLC and KJFK

Airport code mapping:

- `KSLC` is the ICAO code for Salt Lake City International Airport
- `KJFK` is the ICAO code for John F. Kennedy International Airport
- `SLC` and `JFK` are the more common IATA passenger-facing codes
