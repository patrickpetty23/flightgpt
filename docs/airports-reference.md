# Airports Reference

## Salt Lake City International Airport

- IATA: `SLC`
- ICAO: `KSLC`
- Location: Salt Lake City, Utah
- Notes: major Delta hub and key airport for Mountain West travel

## John F. Kennedy International Airport

- IATA: `JFK`
- ICAO: `KJFK`
- Location: New York City, New York
- Notes: major international gateway with heavy long-haul traffic

## Los Angeles International Airport

- IATA: `LAX`
- ICAO: `KLAX`
- Location: Los Angeles, California
- Notes: one of the busiest US airports and a major Pacific gateway

## Chicago O'Hare International Airport

- IATA: `ORD`
- ICAO: `KORD`
- Location: Chicago, Illinois
- Notes: major United and American hub with frequent weather-related delays

## Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport

- IATA: `ATL`
- ICAO: `KATL`
- Location: Atlanta, Georgia
- Notes: one of the busiest passenger airports in the world and a major Delta hub

## Dallas Fort Worth International Airport

- IATA: `DFW`
- ICAO: `KDFW`
- Location: Dallas-Fort Worth, Texas
- Notes: major American Airlines hub and a large domestic transfer airport

## Denver International Airport

- IATA: `DEN`
- ICAO: `KDEN`
- Location: Denver, Colorado
- Notes: large hub with strong domestic connectivity and weather impact in winter
